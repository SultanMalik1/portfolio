City Cares Locator

City Cares Locator is a full-stack web application designed to assist individuals with unstable housing in NYC. Leveraging technologies such as React for the frontend and Supabase for the backend, the platform empowers users to discover and connect with over 100 organizations tailored to their specific needs. From shelters and employment services to support for families, single moms, and the LGBTQ+ community, City Cares Locator provides a comprehensive solution for finding nearby organizations that address unique challenges.
